Eight One Nine Two

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Eight One Nine Two (“the Game”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or phone number.

The game may automatically collect some data for analytics and advertising purposes:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID)
  • App usage statistics (screens viewed, session duration)
  • Crash reports and performance data

All data is collected anonymously and is used to improve app functionality and deliver relevant ads.

2. Local Data Storage

The app uses Flutter Secure Storage to store data locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress
  • User preferences
  • Settings

No personal or account information is stored.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game may use third-party services that collect and process information. These include:

  • Google Mobile Ads / AdMob – for serving advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics – for app usage tracking and crash reporting

These services may collect device information and advertising identifiers. You can review their privacy policies through the links above.

4. Advertising

Eight One Nine Two uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use:

  • Device identifiers (like Google Advertising ID)
  • Cookies
  • Usage data

This enables personalized or non-personalized ads. Users can opt out of personalized ads in device settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

6. Data Safety Compliance

  • All data collected is anonymous and used for ads and analytics.
  • No PII is collected.
  • Users can opt out of personalized ads.
  • The game is not targeted at children under 13.

7. Security

We strive to use commercially reasonable measures to protect information stored locally or processed by third-party services. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at the URL provided in Play Console.
